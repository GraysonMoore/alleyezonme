List of Song Recommendations


Tupac's "All Eyez On Me" has songs for every vibe, which allows him to reach audiences of all types. Whether you're looking for something to play at a party, on a late-night drive, or are in your feelings, Tupac has the song for you. This album solidified him as one of the greatest of all time. Many songs also reflect the metamodernist vibes, with songs like "D4 Life," "Triumphant," and "Life Goes On." These highlight the importance of overcoming the chaos of life and maintaining hope.
